About Us

Step into the world of vintage aesthetics at our coffee shop, established since 70+ years. Our walls are adorned with unique, retro decor that transports you to another era. With cosy seating and a relaxed atmosphere, you'll be able to unwind and enjoy a cup of coffee in style.

We pride ourselves on offering a great variety of options. Our coffee menu features classic blends, as well as unique combinations that will tantalise your taste buds. We also have a selection of teas, smoothies, and pastries to complement your coffee. At our coffee shop, we aim to provide the perfect environment for visitors to enjoy a delicious drink, chat with friends, or work in a peaceful setting.
